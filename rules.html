Rules & Guidelines

  • The contest starts at 0000 hours on 11th August, 2014
  • The contest ends as soon as three people successfully clear the final level or as the Chakravyuha team deems fit. The participants will be given due notice of this.
  • At the end of the contest, the three top positions in the leaderboard go on to win the prizes unless found using unacceptable methods or cheating. In this case, the position and prizes will be given to whoever is next on the leaderboard.
  • Participation in this event is solely individual and anyone found participating in a team will be disqualified.
  • Any attempts at malpractice would result in deletion of the contestant's account.
  • Any kind of automation is not permitted and will lead to deletion and a permanent ban.
  • Hints for each level may hide anywhere in the page.
  • The format of the answers is all lowercase, no spaces and usually will not contain special characters. For example, if you think the answer to level 0 is Leonardo DiCaprio, submit leonardodicaprio.
  • Morpheus' decision is final and binding.
